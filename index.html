Services

I specialize in creating full-stack web applications and designing on-brand, aesthetic websites. I take pride in and am meticulous in my work. Maintaining excellent communication and rapport with my clients is of the utmost importance. To maintain clear expectations and an understanding of each of our needs, my work takes place in four phases.

  • Consultation - We will start with an in-depth conversation about your needs and explore how to best implement those needs.

  • Proposal - I will promptly provide a detailed proposal based on your budget and needs.

  • Development - You will receive regularly scheduled updates regarding the status of the project during design, development and testing.

  • Launch & Support - Once the website has passed testing and client review, it's time to launch! For peace of mind, I offer a regular update package for ongoing support.
